About me

Just the highlights

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas, where I'm diving deeper into my passion for technology and problem-solving. I completed my undergraduate studies at IIT Madras, earning a dual degree in Biological Sciences and Data Science, which provided me with a unique interdisciplinary perspective on both life sciences and computational challenges.

During my time at IIT Madras, I gained valuable industry experience by working with organizations such as Digitus Business Solutions, PricewaterhouseCoopers, and The Jackson Laboratory. At Digitus, I developed cutting-edge solutions like a text-to-SQL chatbot and a RAG-based document QA system. My internship at PricewaterhouseCoopers allowed me to hone my skills in data analytics, where I worked on customer segmentation and sales forecasting, integrating advanced Python solutions and API development. At The Jackson Laboratory, I engaged in innovative research, focusing on temporal video segmentation and action recognition using machine learning techniques.

At IIT Madras, I played an active role in organizing and participating in numerous coding-related activities. From leading nationwide programming contests to conducting workshops, I've always enjoyed sharing my knowledge and contributing to the growth of the coding community.

As I continue my journey in the field of computer science, I'm excited about the possibilities ahead and look forward to leveraging my skills and experiences to make meaningful impact.
